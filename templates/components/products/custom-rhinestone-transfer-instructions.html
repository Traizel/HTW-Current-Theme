Requesting a Quote

In order to receive a quote, please use the Request a Quote button found in the description area of this page. We will need the following information to complete the quote:

  • Contact information
  • Image of artwork.
    • Acceptable formats: JPG file, PDF file, Adobe Illustrator file, EPS file
  • Color of preferred stones
  • Dimensions of design in Width (w) and Height (h)
    • 14x14 inch maximum
  • Quantity of transfers
    • 10 transfers minimum
