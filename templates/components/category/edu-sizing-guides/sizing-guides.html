GARMENT SIZE MATTERS

The size of your garment definitely plays a role in the size of your artwork. With every garment we always recommend measuring with a ruler so you have a better visualization of how the dimensions will work on the garment. The shirt size can be different from brand to brand. Below are size examples for you to try on your garments.

Lay a sheet of printer
paper on the garment to
get a quick visual of how
an 8.5 x 11 size will look
on your garment. For
smaller designs, you
could print off your design
for more visual
representation

When keeping the scale
of the design
proportional, take the
largest width or height
you can go on your
garment and keep the
opposing measurement
linked together in your
software.



Try placing 3 fingers
horizontally from the
collar to help
with spacing
your design




Before you press your
transfer on the garment,
lightly fold the transfer in
half too help gauge the
middle.
